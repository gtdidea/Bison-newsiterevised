# TheBisonGroup.io Website Development Plan

## Overview
Professional consultancy website showcasing The Bison Group's expertise in operational excellence, AI integration, and business transformation.

## Web Design Style
- **Layout Style**: Modern, professional corporate design with clean sections and clear hierarchy
- **Visual Elements**: Hero banner, service cards, methodology diagrams, testimonial sections
- **Color Scheme**: Professional blue and gray tones with white backgrounds, accent colors for CTAs
- **Typography**: Clean, readable fonts with clear headings and body text hierarchy

- `src/pages/Index.tsx` - Main homepage with all sections
- `index.html` - Update title and meta information
